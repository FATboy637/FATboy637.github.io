Our Services

At Computer Hub, we offer a wide range of professional IT services tailored for home users, small businesses, and gamers. Whether you need technical support, security solutions, or performance-driven machines, our team is ready to assist with fast, friendly, and affordable service.

  • IT Support: Reliable on-site or off-site assistance for software issues, hardware setup, system troubleshooting, and ongoing maintenance to keep your systems running smoothly.
  • Remote Support: Fast, secure remote assistance for quick fixes or emergency troubleshooting—perfect when you need help without waiting for a visit.
  • CCTV Installation: Professional installation of custom surveillance systems for homes and businesses, including remote monitoring and integration for complete security solutions.
  • Laptop & Computer Repairs: Affordable repair services for all major brands, covering power issues, cracked screens, component upgrades, and more to extend the life of your devices.
  • Custom Gaming PCs: High-performance, built-to-order PCs for gaming, streaming, or demanding applications—fully tailored to your needs, preferences, and budget.
  • Website Development Expertly designed and developed websites that are responsive, modern, and user-friendly. We create everything from simple landing pages to full-featured e-commerce platforms, customized to your vision and business goals.

Got a question or need help? We're here to assist! Send us a message on WhatsApp or give us a call — whether it’s for a quick quote, tech support, or service booking, we’re ready to help.
