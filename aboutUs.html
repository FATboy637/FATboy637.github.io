About Us

Welcome to Computer Hub, your trusted source for high-quality refurbished laptops, computers, gaming PCs, and accessories. Founded by Pierre Bezuidenhout, our mission is to make reliable technology accessible and affordable for everyone. We understand that brand-new devices can be costly, which is why we specialize in refurbished products that offer the same performance at a fraction of the price. Every item we sell is carefully tested and restored to ensure top-notch quality, giving you the best value for your money.

At Computer Hub, we believe that everyone deserves access to dependable technology without breaking the bank. Whether you're a student, gamer, professional, or small business owner, our wide range of budget-friendly computers and parts is designed to meet your needs. Customer satisfaction is at the heart of what we do, and we’re committed to providing excellent service, expert advice, and great deals. Shop with confidence at computerhub.co.za and experience affordable technology with peace of mind!

Our Philosophy

At Computer Hub, we believe that technology should be powerful, reliable, and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide high-quality refurbished laptops, computers, gaming PCs, and accessories at prices that make sense—without compromising on performance. We’re committed to sustainability, affordability, and innovation, ensuring that every device we sell is tested, optimized, and built to last.

At Computer Hub, we don’t just sell tech—we empower people with performance-driven, budget-friendly, and eco-conscious solutions for work, play, and everything in between.
